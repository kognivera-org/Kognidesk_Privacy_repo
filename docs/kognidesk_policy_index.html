1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Kognivera Technologies Private Limited (“Kognivera”, “we”, “us”, or “our”) collects, uses, stores, and protects information in connection with the Kognidesk mobile application (the “App”). Kognidesk is a front-office visitor management application used by Kognivera and its authorised partner organisations to register, track, and manage visitor entries at their office premises.

By installing or using Kognidesk, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with this policy, please do not use the App.

2. Scope

Kognidesk is intended for use by authorised front-office staff (“Operators”) of organisations that have licensed the application. Operators use the App to record information about individuals (“Visitors”) who arrive at the licensee’s premises. This policy covers both Operator data and Visitor data handled through the App.

3. Information We Collect

3.1 Information about Operators (App Users)

When an Operator logs in to use Kognidesk, we collect:

  • Account credentials — username and password provisioned by the licensee organisation.
  • Role & permissions — the operator’s assigned role (e.g., reception, agent, administrator) and active status.
  • Assigned office location — the branch or facility the operator is assigned to.

3.2 Information about Visitors (Captured by Operators)

When a Visitor checks in through Kognidesk, an Operator may capture the following on the Visitor’s behalf:

  • Identity details — full name, phone number, email address.
  • Visitor photograph — a still image captured at the time of entry for identification purposes.
  • Origin location — the city/address the Visitor is travelling from (free-text; no GPS coordinates are captured).
  • Visit purpose — reason for visit, type of service, agency or company represented, point of contact, recipient name(s), description, bill/reference numbers, and item quantity where applicable.
  • Visit timestamps — date and time of check-in (“timeIn”) and check-out (“timeOut”) along with the office timezone.
  • Office location of visit — the office branch where the visit occurred.
  • Marketing consent — a yes/no indicator if the Visitor consents to receive promotional communication.
  • Visit history — aggregate count of prior visits associated with the Visitor record.

3.3 Information We Do Not Collect

  • We do not collect precise GPS or geolocation coordinates from the device.
  • We do not use third-party advertising SDKs, ad identifiers (IDFA/AAID), or behavioural tracking.
  • We do not integrate third-party analytics SDKs (e.g., Firebase Analytics, Google Analytics, Mixpanel) within the App.
  • We do not collect contacts, calendar, microphone, SMS, or browsing history from the device.
  • We do not collect health, biometric, or financial information.

3.4 Technical Information

Our backend servers maintain standard operational logs that may include application-level events, error messages, and stack traces for diagnostic purposes. These logs do not include device advertising identifiers and are retained only for as long as required for service health monitoring.

4. How Information Is Collected

  • Directly from the Operator — via login screens and visitor registration forms within the App.
  • From the Visitor, through the Operator — details verbally provided by a Visitor and entered into the App by the Operator.
  • From the device camera — a still photograph of the Visitor is captured only when the Operator triggers the camera within the registration flow. Camera access is requested through the device’s standard permission prompt.
  • Automatically by the App backend — check-in/check-out timestamps are generated by the system at the moment of entry or exit.

6. How Information Is Used and Shared

6.1 Internal Use

Collected information is used solely to operate the App, enable visitor check-in/check-out workflows, generate periodic visitor reports for the licensee’s administrators, and ensure security of the premises.

6.2 Sharing with Third Parties

We do not sell, rent, or trade personal information. We share data only with the following processors that support the App’s operation:

  • Google Cloud Platform — Cloud Storage: visitor photographs are stored in a private Google Cloud Storage bucket controlled by Kognivera. Access is restricted to authenticated server-side processes and time-limited signed URLs.
    Google Cloud Privacy Notice
  • Microsoft Graph API (Office 365): used to deliver periodic visitor reports as emailed attachments to designated administrators of the licensee organisation. Microsoft processes only the email envelope and report attachment necessary to deliver the message.
    Microsoft Privacy Statement
  • MongoDB-based database hosting: visitor and operator records are stored in a Kognivera-controlled database instance hosted on enterprise cloud infrastructure with restricted network access.

6.3 Disclosure for Legal Reasons

We may disclose information if required to do so by law, regulation, court order, or a valid request by a public authority, or where necessary to protect the rights, property, or safety of Kognivera, our users, or others.

7. Data Retention and Deletion

  • Operator records are retained for the duration of the operator’s engagement with the licensee organisation and are deactivated upon termination.
  • Visitor records and visit entries are retained for up to twenty-four (24) months from the date of the most recent visit, after which they are eligible for deletion or anonymisation, subject to any longer retention required by applicable law or by the licensee’s internal compliance policy.
  • Visitor photographs follow the same retention schedule as the associated visitor record.
  • Backend operational logs are retained for up to ninety (90) days for diagnostic purposes.

Visitors or Operators may request deletion of their personal data by contacting us at the address in Section 11. Verified deletion requests will be honoured within thirty (30) days, subject to any overriding legal or contractual retention requirement.

8. Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and organisational measures designed to protect personal data, including:

  • Transport-layer encryption (HTTPS/TLS) for all communication between the App and our backend servers.
  • JSON Web Token (JWT) based authentication with session expiry to control access to backend APIs.
  • Role-based access control limiting data visibility to authorised personnel within the licensee organisation.
  • Storage of visitor photographs in a private cloud storage bucket protected by signed, short-lived access URLs.
  • Network-level access restrictions on database infrastructure.

No method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect personal data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction (including under the EU General Data Protection Regulation and the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (India)), you may have the following rights with respect to personal data we hold about you:

  • Right of access — to request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Right of rectification — to request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Right of erasure — to request deletion of your personal data, subject to legal retention requirements.
  • Right to withdraw consent — including withdrawal of marketing consent at any time.
  • Right to object or restrict processing in certain circumstances.
  • Right to lodge a complaint with the relevant data protection authority in your jurisdiction.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the details in Section 11.

10. Children’s Privacy

Kognidesk is intended for use by adult professional Operators in business front-office settings. It is not directed at children under 13 years of age, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child’s information has been provided to us, please contact us so that we can take appropriate action to remove it.
